Liquid and Gas Thermal Properties

This topic lists the various thermal properties of common liquids and gases, focusing on those you might use for heat transfer.

Liquids

Liquids are commonly used in liquid pipe loops for heat transfer. You might also submerge objects in liquids for additional heat capacity, such as a thermo aquatuner in a steam box for steam turbines.

Note that super coolant requires rocketry, making it a late game material.

Gases

Gases can be used in gas pipe loops for heat transfer. More commonly, they are an ambient atmosphere that heat is dispersed in.
