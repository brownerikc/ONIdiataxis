Material Thermal Properties

This topic lists the various thermal properties of materials and what you can construct from them. Some exceptions exist for liquid pipes.

Most materials listed can be obtained early in the game, but some require more effort and advanced technology. Materials requiring extra effort are marked as follows:

  • Manufactured: Obtainable with moderate research and setup (approximately mid-game)
  • Space: Obtainable through advanced research and rocketry (approximately late game)
